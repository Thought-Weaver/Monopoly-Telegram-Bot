Hello! This bot was made to let people play Monopoly in Telegram. In order to use me, you'll need to add me to a chat and have everyone who wishes to play directly message me (t.me/LA_Uno_Game_Bot).

Commands:

/newgame - Begins a new game of Monopoly.
/rules - Sends a message with the rules of Monopoly.
/help - Sends a message with additional commands and information.
